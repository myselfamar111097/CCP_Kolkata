From Principle's Desk

Polytechnic education in West Bengal is on a springboard of development. There are activities galore and the entire system is bubbling with excitement. New government polytechnics are being set up in the unserved sub-divisions of the State in a rapid pace. Many more are on the cards for which preparatory work is going on in full swing.
This would not have been possible but for the active interest and deep commitment shown by many from the system. As it was not feasible earlier to thank all of them individually, I take this opportunity to compliment every single individual of the polytechnic system who has responded responsibly and accomplished the task given at a short notice. In fact, some of them exhibited exemplary enthusiasm and sincerity. I was indeed impressed, if not overwhelmed, by the quality of work dished out and by the dedication manifest in their attitude.
Yet, as the poet said, though the woods are lovely, dark and deep...
