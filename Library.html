Our Library

    As of December 2016, Central Calcutta Polytechnic offers Departmental books for :

        Diploma books for Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science & Technology as well as Electronics and Telecommunications.
        Post-Diploma books for Medical Electronics.

    The readers are given access to library books irrespective of their Departments. The names of the books are provided to the librarian by the students which are then taken out of the shelves and provided to the students.

  Funding

    Our college library is funded by the State Council.
    Over the past 1 year, more than 11,800 books has been added to our library so that students can take any departmental books they want unperturbed.

  Issue of Library Card

    The following documents are required for issuing a library card to a student:

        Money Receipt (Original + 1 Photocopy)
        Two color passport pictures.

  Rules and Regulations:

        Students must maintain silence at all times.
        For 1st year students, 2 books can be issued per week. For 2nd and 3rd year students, 3 books can be issued per week.
        Reading room must be kept clean at all times.
        Students must take care of the books taken from the library.
        Damaged books won't be taken back.
        Students must bear the cost of misplaced or lost or damaged books.
        All the books taken in a particular year must be returned and cleared otherwise the concerned student wont get his/her results.
        No food items must be taken inside the reading room.
        All the switches must be switched off while exiting the library.

  Reading Services

    The Library of our college possesses a well-equipped and spacious reading room with a seasting capacity of 20-25 students.
