Eligibility

Only Jexpo rank holder Students Can Apply for this institute.

    JEXPO

JEXPO is a state level entrance exam which is also known as WB Polytechnic. Joint Entrance Examination for Polytechnic is administered by the West Bengal State Council of Technical Education. Candidates can get admission in the government and private aided polytechnic institutions of the state. It is organized to screen the candidates those wishing to apply in the first year of Diploma courses in Engineering & Technology in the state. From this article, candidates can access the complete information webscte.org site.

    Nationality

  Candidates must be an Indian citizen.

    State Eligibilities:

      Candidates those have the domicile of West Bengal state can apply.
      Those studied their 10th examination from WB can also apply.
      Other state candidates are also eligible to apply under outside state candidates.

    Qualifying Exam:

      Candidate must qualify or appearing in the SSC (high school/10th) or equivalent exam from any of the recognized board.

    Age Criteria::

      Minimum age:  The minimum age for applying is 15 years.
      Maximum age:  There is no maximum age for applying.

    Qualifying Marks:

      It is mandatory to score at least 35% marks in aggregate without the marks of an additional subject (without rounding up) in the qualifying examination.
      Candidate must acquire at least pass marks in all the three subjects as per their secondary board.

      Note

  Without rounding up means that any shortfall of marks from the above benchmark will render the candidate ineligible for JEXPO (Example: 34.99% marks will not be considered as 35%).

    Appearing Candidates:

      Those are studying of their 10th class can also apply.
      Candidates, those results are awaited or yet be declared can are also eligible to apply.
